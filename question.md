Deploying a Production-Ready Model Using MLOps
Project Goal
Deploy a student dropout prediction model 

UCI "Predict Students' Dropout and Academic Success"
•	Link: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/697/predict+students+dropout+and+academic+success
•	Kaggle Mirror: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/thedevastator/higher-education-predictors-of-student-retention


Rationale — Why This Approach
Why this problem: Zimbabwean universities face high dropout rates with no data-driven early warning tools. This system predicts which students are at risk and recommends specific interventions (e.g., financial aid referral, tutoring assignment). It is directly relevant to UZ and adaptable to any African university.
